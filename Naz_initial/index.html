Workshops

Canvas painting
In this workshop, participants had the opportunity to express their artistic talents on canvas. From blending colors to creating their own unique artwork, attendees will receive guidance and inspiration from the instructor to unleash their creative potential.
Embroidery
This workshop offered a creative space for participants to explore the art of embroidery. Under the guidance of an experienced instructor, attendees learnt various embroidery techniques, stitches, and patterns to create beautiful and intricate designs on fabric.
Lippan art
Lippan art is a traditional Indian form of mural decoration. In this workshop, Manjusha Ma'am will guide participants in creating intricate mud and mirror artwork, allowing them to experience the rich cultural heritage of this craft.
Tote bag painting
This workshop focused on personalizing tote bags through creative painting techniques. Shefali guided participants in transforming ordinary tote bags into unique and stylish accessories by exploring various painting methods.
Art therapy
Art therapy, facilitated by M Power and Aditya Birla, offers a therapeutic outlet through creative expression. Participants engaged in art activities that promote mental well-being, self-reflection, and emotional healing.
Relief printing
In this workshop, attendees explored the art of relief printing. Gunjan Ma'am taught participants how to create intricate designs by carving a block and then printing it onto paper or fabric, resulting in unique and textured prints.
